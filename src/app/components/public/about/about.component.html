ABOUT US

Passionate About Automotive Excellence

KRH Auto Body isn’t just a shop—it’s a place where craftsmanship, care, and community come together. From daily drivers to dream builds, we treat every vehicle like it’s our own.

At KRH Auto Body, we bring vehicles back to life with precision collision repair, custom paint work, and full restoration services. With decades of hands-on experience behind every repair, our team blends old-school craftsmanship with modern tools and techniques.

We’re obsessed with the details—panel gaps, paint depth, how the reflections look under shop lights, and how the car feels when you finally pick it up. Whether it’s a minor fender-bender or a full restoration project, our goal is simple: you should feel proud every time you see your vehicle.

From working directly with insurance companies to helping you understand repair options, we’re here to make the process clear, honest, and stress-free. When your car is in our care, it’s in the hands of people who genuinely love what they do.
